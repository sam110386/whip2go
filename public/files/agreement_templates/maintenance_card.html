Tire Rotation
Rotate 4 tires according to manufacturers recommendations. Check tire
pressure and adjust to manufacturers recommendations. Visually inspect:
tires (for uneven wear/damage), suspension, struts, shocks, bushings,
springs, steering rack & pump, and drive train components for wear/leaks.

Oil Change
Replace Oil Filter & Engine Oil (Synthetic Oil only if required). Lubricate
all fittings and hinges. Top off all fluid levels as needed. Examine all belts
& hoses. Check & adjust air pressure in all tires, including spare. Check
operation of horn, exterior lights, turn signals & hazard warning lights.
Check windshield washer spray & wiper operation. If any additional parts
are necessary, contact CCM for approval.
